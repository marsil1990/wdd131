Site Plan - Club Nacional de Football Fan Website

Site Name

Name: Nacional Fan Page

This name reflects the purpose of the site: a place for fans of Club Nacional de football to find information the team, players, and its legacy.

Optional domain: nacionalfans.com (not required to purchase)

Site Purpose

This is dedicated to providing fans with news about the team, a current player roster, a summary of the club's rich history, and a way to connect through a fan form.

Scenarios

  • When is Nacional's next match?
  • Who are the current players and their positions?
  • How can I share who my favorite player is?

Color Schema

  • Primary color: #002f6c (dark blue) - used for header, footer, and text highlights.
  • Accent color: #d9002c (red) - used for headings and important visual elements.
  • Background: #ffffff (white) - for general page background to keep good contrast.

Typography

  • Primary Fonts: Roboto and Open Sans (from Google Fonts)
  • Usage:
    • Headings (h1, h2, etc.): Roboto (bold weights)
    • Body text, paragraphs, labels, forms: Open Sans (regular)
    • Buttons and UI elements: Open Sans (medium/bold)
